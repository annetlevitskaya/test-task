About

Spekl specializes in the production of card products for various purposes, as well as SIM cards packaging for mobile operators and kits with cards for retail companies, banks, insurance companies and others. The company was founded in 1998 in Kyiv (Ukraine) and at the moment collaborates with customers in Europe, Africa and Asia.

Spekl is certified according to ISO 9001:2008. Production capacity: plastic cards - 26 million per year, cardboard cards (scratch cards) - 150 million per year, SIM card packaging and welcome and gift cards packages production - 400 million per year, data entry - 24 million per year.
